LinkedIn Course: A Standalone Project: Perform Data Analytics to Find Trends through Visualization

Project brief
In this skill-building project, you will analyze real-world data from Airbnb, identify trends from the data, and use data visualization techniques to present your findings. The project is designed to be completed in approximately eight hours.

Airbnb is an online marketplace that connects people looking for affordable, short-term accommodations with hosts offering their properties for rent. Operating in numerous locations worldwide, Airbnb has become a popular choice for those seeking a home-like stay at a more affordable price point than hotels. 

This project focuses on analyzing data from Airbnb to gain insights into pricing trends, location-based variations, and other factors influencing Airbnb listings. Your tasks will include data loading, data cleaning, data transformation, exploratory data analysis, and data visualization. Building on your existing knowledge of data analytics, you will progress through a structured series of steps that guide you from initial data cleaning to detailed analysis and visualization. 

As you progress, you will:

• Clean and preprocess Airbnb data to prepare it for analysis.
• Analyze trends in Airbnb listings based on factors such as pricing, location, and property type.
• Apply data visualization techniques to communicate trends and insights effectively.
• Summarize the impact of factors like location, amenities, and guest reviews on booking trends.

By completing this project, you will gain hands-on experience in analyzing data and extracting meaningful insights, just as you would in a real-world business environment. You can showcase this project in your portfolio to highlight your data analysis and visualization skills. It is a great way to strengthen your profile.

**Prerequisite knowledge

Before starting this project, you should have a foundational understanding of key concepts and tools related to data analytics and visualization. Specifically, you should be familiar with the following:

*Data concepts: A good understanding of fundamental data concepts such as data types (e.g., numeric, categorical), data cleaning, and data storage is essential.

*Python programming: You should be comfortable with basic Python syntax, including variables, loops, conditionals, and functions. You should also have experience with libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.

*Data analysis principles: A solid understanding of data analysis principles, including introductory statistical methods, will help you in analyzing and interpreting datasets effectively.

*Data visualization: Familiarity with data visualization techniques is crucial for presenting insights clearly. Knowledge of tools like Tableau and Power BI is not mandatory but can enhance your ability to present insights interactively.

*Relational databases and SQL: An introductory understanding of relational database management systems (RDBMS) and how to query databases using SQL is important.

If you are not fully confident in any of these areas, you can review relevant resources or take a foundational course on the topic before proceeding with the project. This will ensure you have the necessary knowledge to complete the tasks successfully.

**About the dataset

This project uses the Airbnb Open Data dataset, which contains information about Airbnb listings. It includes information such as host details, neighborhood and location information, property characteristics (e.g., room type, price, availability), review scores, service fees, cancellation policies, and house rules.

The dataset is provided in CSV format and is intended for analyzing Airbnb data to extract meaningful insights.

You can access and download the dataset from the following Kaggle link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/arianazmoudeh/airbnbopendata 

**Business goals

Imagine you are a data analyst at Airbnb.  Your role is to analyze property listings to help the company better understand its offerings and enhance its services. Your focus is on the following business goals:

Pricing optimization: Identify trends in neighborhood pricing and room types to help Airbnb and hosts adjust rates for maximum profitability.

Service improvement: Identify factors such as cancellation policy, reviews, and cleaning fees that affect host performance.
